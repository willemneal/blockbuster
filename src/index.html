Hello, you have got a token!

You'll need to sign in to call contract methods:

Here is how you use can use it from JavaScript.

To init:

        contract.init({initialOwner: nearlib.dev.myAccountId})

To check your balance:

        contract.balanceOf({tokenOwner: nearlib.dev.myAccountId})

To send money:

        contract.transfer({to: 'bob.near', tokens: '1000'})
